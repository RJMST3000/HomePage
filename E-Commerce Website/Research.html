RESEARCH

For the development of website, different appealing websites were researched. Varieties of pecular and attractive components were found during the research. All those components were implemented in our website in different webpages. Five references that we took for the styling and design of our webpages are illustrated below:

Reference 1

In easternleaf website, font used in the navigation bar of the website seems perfect and appealing for a navigation bar of our website.The font used in the navigation bar is 'Lato',sans-serif.So, the font was downloaded and used in the navigation bar of our website.

Reference 2

In adidas products page, whenever the product box is hovered, the black border appears in the product box. This hover effect seems so satisfying and excellent. So, the same hover effect was implemented in the product box of products page of our website.

Reference 3

The layout and design of the contact us page of marvelapp website appears appealing and likable by the customers. The form box in the left with image in the right layout seems so perfect. So, the layout of the contact us page of marvelapp website was used as a reference for our contact us page.

Reference 4

The welcome heading of codewithharry website looks unique and creative. The use of black color for welcome to and an another suitable color for name of the website is fascinating. So, the same coloring was applied to our home page heading 'WELCOME TO SHIZEN BONSAI'. 'WELCOME TO' font was colored black and 'SHIZEN BONSAI'(name of the website) was colored green.

Reference 5

The layout of the collections page of ryderlabel website seems pretty well structured and perfect.So,this layout was used as a refernce for the products page of our website 'Shizen Bonsai'.
